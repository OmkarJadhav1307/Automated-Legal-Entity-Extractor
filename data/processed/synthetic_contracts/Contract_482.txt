SUBCONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made on January 13, 2025 by and between Global Vertex Corporation and Nexus
Titan Holdings.

Confidential information disclosed under this Agreement shall be maintained in strict confidence.

The parties agree that this Agreement shall become effective as of 01/18/2025.

The term of this Agreement shall continue until March 06, 2027, unless earlier terminated pursuant to
its provisions.

Each party shall act in good faith and perform its obligations in accordance with commercially
reasonable standards.

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Texas.

The total consideration payable under this Agreement shall be $2,139,289, subject to applicable taxes
and payment terms.
