STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

. This Agreement is made on October 31, 2017 by and between Apex Summit Holdings and Summit
Redwood Corporation.

. The total consideration payable under this Agreement shall be $1,678,731, subject to applicable
taxes and payment terms.

. The parties agree that this Agreement shall become effective as of 11/21/2017.

. Each party shall act in good faith and perform its obligations in accordance with commercially
reasonable standards.

. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of
Texas.

. The term of this Agreement shall continue until 2019-08-09, unless earlier terminated pursuant to its
provisions.

. Confidential information disclosed under this Agreement shall be maintained in strict confidence.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement.

Apex Summit Holdings
Summit Redwood Corporation
