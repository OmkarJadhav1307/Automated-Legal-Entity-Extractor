SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made on 2017-12-27 by and between Everest Pioneer Ltd. and Pioneer Vertex LLC.

The parties agree that this Agreement shall become effective as of January 11, 2018.

Each party shall act in good faith and perform its obligations in accordance with commercially
reasonable standards.

The total consideration payable under this Agreement shall be $6,322,476, subject to applicable taxes
and payment terms.

The term of this Agreement shall continue until April 29, 2019, unless earlier terminated pursuant to its
provisions.

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of
Delaware.

Confidential information disclosed under this Agreement shall be maintained in strict confidence.
